About Me

I am a graduate of the Coding Bootcamp at UCLA Extension currently pursuing a career in web development. During my time in the Coding Bootcamp at UCLA, I was immersed in a full stack development course starting with JavaScript, CSS, and html and learning to integrate those languages with NodeJS and databases including MongoDB, MySQL and Firebase.

I used the MVC software design pattern to create full stack applications, culminating in the MEAN (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular 1, NodeJS) stack application I created for my final project. I worked collaboratively on three projects with fellow classmates that are further discussed in my resume, and independently on over twenty individual projects.

I believe in the power of these new technologies to influence behavior, provide solutions, and disrupt outdated processes in a positive way. Please take a look at my portfolio below.
